Quick Cinnamon rolls

A quick recipe for delicious Cinnamon rolls for all the family

Quick and easy cinnamon rolls from scratch, with no yeast, proofing, or kneading necessary!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cub butter, divided (Optional)

Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softned (Optional)
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Filling

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Cream cheese Frosting

  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar (Optional)
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened (Optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
